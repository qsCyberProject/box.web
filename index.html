    About

    Box.Web is a fast modern framework, designed to reduce the amount of complexity in your code – meaning that you can focus on optimising your site design. Box.Web is still in its earliest stages – a lot of cool functions are coming soon.

    Updates

    Box.Web is going to be updated on an irregular basis, but we will list functions that will be coming:

    • box.web.js, a bit of JS is required to make better animations.
    • Animations, of course ever page needs animations to attract attention
